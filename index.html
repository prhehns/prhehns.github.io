Why incoming Senior High school students must choose a track?

Prince Malatuba

11-Excellence


After 4 years of studying in Junior High school, here comes Senior High School which is another 2 years of studying. Even though it's just for 2 years, don't underestimate it. Since Senior High School is the place where they teach you essential basic knowledge needed before you enter college.

PHOTO CREDIT: MENDO_VIDEO_PRODUCTIONS VIA VISUALHUNT.COM / CC B

Before enter Senior High School you must choose among the following: Academic, Technical Vocational Livekihood, Sports and Art’s & Design tracks. Under Academic track, there are 4 trands: General Academic (GA) Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMMS) Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM).


Academic track appeals for people who want to pursue college courses. HUMSS for those who like social communication, STEM for those want to pursue Science and Mathematics realated field, ABM for those who pursue business and management related field, GAS if you are not yet sure what to pick a career path. Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track for those who want to work after graduating Senior High School. Arts and Design for those who have creativity. Sport Track for those who want to be an athlete or a coach.


It is important to pick your track so that you will not waste money and time. In picking Be sure of your personality and life goals so to match your taste and experience. Research and Analyze the subject included in each course to help you make your choice. Check schools nearby that offer your choosen track once you’ve set your mind, find out schools nearby that offer the program. Schools in the Philippines adopted the SHS program since 2016, so you need not look far to find the right one for you.

Students get support from DepEd through vouchers for JHS finishers to help them enroll in the most ideal senior high school track. These checks, though, are usable even in specialty schools as long as they adhere to the senior high school curriculum required by the government. As a result, SHS students relish in the opportunity to pursue innovative courses such as animation and ICT, which were otherwise non-existent in the old curriculum.

Your senior high school track directs you towards reaching your goals, so make sure you pick a program that’s most useful to your skills and interests. Further, when you mix self-mastery and ample research, you reach the most fruitful choice for your senior high school studies. Therefore, heed these tips that will guide you to the right path towards your dream career.
